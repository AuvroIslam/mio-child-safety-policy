Mio - Child Safety Standards

1. Clear Policy Statement

Mio strictly prohibits any content or activity related to child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Such content is absolutely not allowed on our platform.

Any users found sharing, promoting, or engaging in such activities will have their accounts immediately terminated, and we will report the activity to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC), as required by law.

2. Minimum Age Requirement

Mio is intended solely for users aged 18 and older. This app is not designed for or directed at children.

We explicitly prohibit individuals under the age of 18 from registering an account or using the Mio service. Our commitment to this age restriction is reflected in the 18+ badge displayed on our Google Play Store listing.

3. Moderation Practices

We employ a combination of methods to monitor user-generated content (including messages, profile information, photos, and usernames) and enforce our policies:

  • User Reporting: We rely on our user community to report content or behavior that violates our standards.
  • Automated Filtering: We utilize automated tools to detect potentially harmful content or patterns.
  • Manual Review: Our dedicated safety team manually reviews reported content and flagged accounts to ensure compliance.

Content or users found violating our child safety policies will be promptly removed or banned from the platform.

4. User Reporting Mechanism

We empower our users to help maintain a safe environment. If you encounter any content or user that you believe violates our policies, especially concerning child safety or abuse, please use the in-app reporting feature immediately.

Alternatively, you can report specific concerns directly to our moderation team via email at: abuse@mioapp.com. Please provide as much detail as possible to help us investigate.

5. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

Mio is committed to cooperating fully with law enforcement agencies regarding investigations related to CSAM/CSAE. We comply with all applicable laws and legal processes concerning the reporting and handling of such materials and related user activity.

6. Point of Contact (for Google / Law Enforcement)

For inquiries from Google, law enforcement, or other official bodies regarding our safety compliance and practices, please contact us at:

mio.app2025@gmail.com

7. Account and Data Deletion

Users have the right to delete their account and associated data. You can initiate the deletion process directly within the Mio app.

Alternatively, you can request account and data deletion by submitting this form: Data Deletion Request Form.

Requests submitted via the form will be reviewed, and the deletion will be processed within 3 business days.
